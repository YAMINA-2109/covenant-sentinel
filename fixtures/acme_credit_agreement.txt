CREDIT AGREEMENT

Dated as of 12 March 2024

BETWEEN:
ACME INDUSTRIES S.A.S., a societe par actions simplifiee incorporated in France (the "Borrower")
AND
BANQUE LUTECE S.A. (the "Lender")

FACILITY: EUR 50,000,000 SENIOR TERM LOAN FACILITY ("Facility B")

SECTION 1. DEFINITIONS

"EBITDA" means consolidated earnings before interest, tax, depreciation and amortisation of the Group, calculated in accordance with the Accounting Principles.

"LTM EBITDA" means EBITDA for the trailing twelve-month period ending on the relevant Quarter Date, as reconciled in the Borrower's quarterly reporting (alternative performance measures note). Quarterly (three-month) EBITDA figures shall not be used for covenant testing.

"Total Debt" means the aggregate outstanding principal amount of all financial indebtedness of the Group, including bank facilities, notes, bilateral loans and finance leases.

"Net Interest Expense" means interest payable on Total Debt less interest income, measured on a trailing twelve-month basis.

"Leverage Ratio" means, on any Quarter Date, the ratio of Total Debt to LTM EBITDA.

"Interest Coverage Ratio" means, on any Quarter Date, the ratio of LTM EBITDA to Net Interest Expense.

"Liquidity" means unrestricted cash and cash equivalents of the Group.

"Quarter Date" means 31 March, 30 June, 30 September and 31 December in each year.

SECTION 7. COVENANTS

Section 7.1 Financial Covenants. The Borrower shall ensure that:

(a) Leverage Ratio. The Leverage Ratio on each Quarter Date shall not exceed 3.50:1.00.

(b) Minimum Liquidity. Liquidity on each Quarter Date shall not be less than EUR 5,000,000.

(c) Interest Coverage. The Interest Coverage Ratio on each Quarter Date shall not be less than 2.00:1.00.

Section 7.2 Financial Reporting. The Borrower shall deliver to the Lender, within 45 days after each Quarter Date, a compliance certificate setting out computations of the financial covenants in reasonable detail.

Section 7.3 Notification of Default. The Borrower shall notify the Lender of any Default, and the steps being taken to remedy it, promptly upon becoming aware of it.

SECTION 9. MISCELLANEOUS

Section 9.4 Measurement; Supersession. Financial covenants are tested by reference to the final figures reported as at the relevant Quarter Date. Preliminary, flash or interim estimates circulated before publication of the final quarter-end figures are superseded by the final figures for all covenant purposes.

SECTION 10. EVENTS OF DEFAULT

Section 10.1 Financial Covenant Default. Failure to comply with any covenant in Section 7.1 constitutes an Event of Default, subject to Section 10.2.

Section 10.2 Equity Cure. The Borrower may cure a breach of Section 7.1(a) no more than twice over the life of the Facility by way of a qualifying equity contribution applied to the reduction of Total Debt within 20 Business Days of delivery of the relevant compliance certificate.
